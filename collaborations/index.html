Our Community Partners

We strive to work with a diverse group of community members who are involved in alleviating healthcare problems in the community or who are impacted by them. We thank our community partners for all the work and effort they have put in to support and continually inspire us!

Coulter Center

Our mentors, Kristin Bush and David Chen, work with the Coulter Center to help UVA Health professionals bring their device ideas to fruition!

Parents Program

The Parents Program have been helping us fund our club's activities and early device development. Their support has allowed us to explore real-world healthcare needs and create meaningful, hands-on solutions.

UVA BME Department

We have received a lot of support from faculty in the UVA BME department including Dr. Shannon Barker, Dr. Shayni Peirce-Cottler, and Dr. Mami Taniuchi.

UVA Department of Orthopaedic Surgery

Collaborations with Dr. Shawn Russell, director of the Motion Analysis and Motor Performance Laboratory regarding human movement problems.

Fried Center

Physical Therapists at the Fried Center are helping us find problems that we can help solve!
