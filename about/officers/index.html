Who We Are

An interdisciplinary team building FISH together

Our officers come from a wide range of academic backgrounds, bringing together interests in engineering, medicine, neuroscience, psychology, design, research, and operations.

That mix of perspectives helps FISH approach healthcare problems more thoughtfully and build a stronger, more collaborative student organization.

What officers support

From internal projects and research to events, partnerships, funding, and member engagement, the officer team helps shape how FISH grows and what opportunities it can offer.
