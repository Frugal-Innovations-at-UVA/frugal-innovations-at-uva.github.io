Our learning philosophy…

We strongly believe in sharing knowledge within our community. Through guest lectures, workshops, and other events we help students learn about the intersections between medicine and engineering. What’s more is we believe that each of you has something to teach us as well! With our support, you can plan your own workshops and events!
