# Welcome to FISH!

We are a new student organization at the University of Virginia dedicated to making low-cost, effective, sustainable medical devices with the goal of increasing accessibility to medical care. We will work on a yearly basis to research, develop, and prototype a medical device which we can then submit to national competitions and conferences.

As an extremely interdisciplinary group, we are always seeking new members from all backgrounds and majors and would love to have you on our team!

Interested? Check out our events tab or send us a message through the Contact form at the bottom of the page and we will get back to you.
